Search Engine Optimization
Given the absolute necessity of an online presence in modern business, it's no surprise that the term "SEO" remains among the most popular Google searches throughout the year. Everyone needs it, but relatively few people know how to approach the subject with any degree of confidence- especially given the fact that proper SEO requires a multi-faceted approach for true success.

While there are several aspects to successful SEO, keywords are one of the most straightforward, and have some of the most significant ramifications for the internal workings of your website. Their purpose is to answer two fundamental questions about your business:

1) What am I offering?
2) What words and phrases are people using in an attempt to find it?

Answering those questions and then formatting the result for each page on your website helps drive targeted traffic, which increases your clickthrough rates and, by association, your direct sales.
